Instructions
Welcome to Pathfinding Visualizer!

This is a visualization tool for pathfinding algorithms. You can choose the algorithm you want to visualize from the menu.

You can also choose the speed of visualization. The speed is in milliseconds.

USAGE:
1. Choose the cell you want to be the start node by left mouse click.
2. Choose the cell you want to be the end node by right mouse click.
3. Draw obstacles by ctrl+left mouse click and drag.
4. Press the button to visualize the algorithm.
5. Press the reset button to reset the algorithm.
6. Press the clear board button to clear the board.
